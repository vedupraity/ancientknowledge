Hanuman Chalisa

Hanumāna Chalisa is a timeless ode to devotion Lord Hanumāna is known for his devotion to Lord Rāma and is considered to be the embodiment of faith, surrender, and devotion. He is one of seven chiranjeevs (immortals) as per Sanātan Dharma. Hanumāna also appears in Mahabharata on Arjuna’s chariot as ‘Dhwaj’ (flag).

Ganesh Chalisa

Lord Ganesha, also known by different names like Ganapati and Vinayaka, is the son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. Ganesha is considered the Lord of all beginnings and the last resort of obstacles. He is worshiped as the god of good fortune, new ventures and knowledge. Ganesh Chalisa is a religious hymn dedicated to Lord Ganesha.

Shiv Chalisa

Reciting this Śiva Cālīsā composed by Saint Ayodhyādāsa early in the morning every day, one can easily earn grace of Lord Śiva which brings happiness, good forture, wealth and knowledge. Lord Śiva is the owner of supernatural power, only from his grace, the person gets away from all the troubles.
