About us

AncientKnowledge.in is an educational and non-profit initiative to build a free digital library of ancient scriptures and texts which were originally composed in the Sanskrit (Saṃskṛta) language, and our goal is to provide the most accurate translation available on the internet with the best reading experience.

We are a small team working on this project with a fair intention to promote the ancient knowledge worldwide and we are looking for extending our team. If you are interested in joining us and want to get along with our journey, contact us at ancientknowledgeinbox@gmail.com

We are now Open-Source and welcoming any contribution to improve our project.

Checkout our github repositories at:

  • Content Repository - ancientknowledgedatabase
  • Website Repository - ancientknowledgewebserver
